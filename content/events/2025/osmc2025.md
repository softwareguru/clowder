At OSMC, you get insights from real monitoring experts, not marketing slides. Developers, admins, and industry leaders share hands-on experience, best practices, and deep technical know-how – helping you solve real-world challenges.

For over 15 years, OSMC has been the go-to event for Open Source Monitoring. With 250+ international attendees each year, it’s the perfect place to connect, share ideas, and collaborate. With tons of networking opportunities, OSMC offers a unique environment to learn and grow together.
